Most famous car around the world

Bugatti chiron

Known for: Insane speed, luxury, and being one of the fastest production cars in the world.

  • Quad-Turbo W16 Engine – 8.0L engine with 1,479 hp.
  • Top Speed – Electronically limited to 420 km/h (261 mph).
  • 0–100 km/h in ~2.4s – Super quick acceleration.
  • Luxury Interior – Premium materials and advanced tech.

Mclaren p1

Known for: Being a hybrid hypercar with cutting-edge tech and track-focused performance.

  1. Hybrid Powertrain – Twin-turbo V8 + electric motor = 903 hp.
  2. Top Speed – Electronically limited to 420 km/h (261 mph).
  3. Limited Edition – Only 375 units made.
  4. Active Aero & Suspension – Designed for both road and track.

Toyota suppra

Known for: Balanced performance, tunability, and connection to JDM culture.

  • Inline-6 Turbo Engine – BMW-sourced 3.0L with 382 hp.
  • Rear-Wheel Drive – Sporty handling and fun to drive.
  • Affordable Sports Car – Great value for performance.
  • Tuning Friendly – Popular among car modders.
